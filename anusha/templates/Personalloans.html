Personal Loan Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a personal loan, you have to meet certain criteria. Below are the important factors that lenders take into consideration to decide your eligibility for a personal loan.

  • Age should fall under the range of 21 years to 60 years.
  • Net monthly income should be 15,000/- for salaried and self-employed, yearly transactions should be a minimum of 20 lakhs.
  • Credit score must be above 650.
  • Employment stability.
  • Maintained a good credit score.
  • Clear repayment history.
  • Must be a Resident Citizen of India.

Personal Loan FAQs

What is a personal loan?
A personal loan is a type of loan offered by financial institutions that allows individuals to borrow a fixed amount of money for various personal expenses.
How can I apply for a personal loan?
You can apply for a personal loan by visiting the lender's branch, filling out an online application form on their website, or through mobile banking apps.
What are the eligibility criteria for a personal loan?
Eligibility criteria typically include age, income, employment status, credit score, and residency status.
What documents are required for a personal loan application?
Commonly required documents include identity proof, address proof, income proof, bank statements, and passport-sized photographs.
How is the interest rate on a personal loan determined?
The interest rate is influenced by factors such as the applicant's creditworthiness, loan amount, loan tenure, and prevailing market rates.
What are the repayment options for a personal loan?
Repayment options typically include Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs) through post-dated cheques, auto-debit from bank accounts, or online payments.
Can I prepay or foreclose a personal loan?
Yes, many lenders allow prepayment or foreclosure of personal loans, but it may be subject to certain terms and conditions, including prepayment charges.
What happens if I miss an EMI payment?
Missing an EMI payment can lead to penalties such as late payment fees, increased interest rates on overdue amounts, and negative impact on credit score.
How long does it take for a personal loan to be approved?
Approval times vary but can typically range from a few hours to a few days, depending on the lender's verification process and documentation requirements.
What should I consider before applying for a personal loan?
Factors to consider include the interest rate, fees and charges, repayment terms, eligibility criteria, and your ability to repay the loan comfortably.
