How to Apply for Gold Loan at Ruloans?

To apply for a gold loan, you can adhere to these typical steps. Note that the exact procedure may differ based on the lender and your location. Here are a few points to remember before applying for a gold loan:

  • Research and Choose a Lender: Research and select a reputable lender offering gold loans. This could be a bank, financial institution, or specialized gold loan company.
  • Review Eligibility Criteria: Examine the lender's eligibility requirements. Typically, you must meet age criteria and possess gold jewelry that meets their standards.
  • Visit Branch or Website: Visit the nearest branch or apply online via the official website or mobile app, if available.
  • Gold Evaluation: If applying in person, bring your gold to the lender's branch for evaluation of purity, weight, and value to determine your eligible loan amount. Some fintech companies also provide doorstep evaluation services.

Gold Loan EMI Calculator

How is Loan Against Property EMI Calculated?
Gold Loan EMI is calculated using the following Compound Interest formula:

$$ \text{EMI} = \frac{P \times r \times (1 + r)^n}{(1 + r)^n - 1} $$


Where:

EMI = Equated Monthly Installment

P = Loan Against Property principal amount

r = Monthly interest rate (Annual interest rate divided by 12, expressed as a decimal)

n = Loan Against Property tenure in months

FAQs

What is a gold loan?
A gold loan is a secured loan where gold jewelry or ornaments are pledged as collateral to avail funds from a lender.
How much loan can I get against my gold?
The loan amount typically ranges from 60% to 80% of the market value of the gold pledged.
What documents are required to apply for a gold loan?
Generally, documents such as identity proof, address proof, and proof of ownership of gold are required.
What happens if I fail to repay the gold loan?
In case of non-repayment, the lender may auction the gold to recover the loan amount.
What are the interest rates for gold loans?
Interest rates for gold loans vary but are generally lower compared to other types of loans, typically ranging from 7% to 29% per annum.
Can I get my gold back after repaying the loan?
Yes, once the loan along with interest and other charges is fully repaid, you can retrieve your pledged gold from the lender.
What happens if the gold's market value changes during the loan tenure?
The loan amount is typically based on the initial market value of the gold at the time of loan disbursal. Any increase or decrease in value generally does not affect the loan terms.
Are there any prepayment charges for gold loans?
Many lenders do not impose prepayment penalties on gold loans, allowing borrowers to repay the loan before the tenure ends without additional charges.
